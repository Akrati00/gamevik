**Steam** is a digital distribution platform developed by Valve Corporation that has transformed the gaming industry. To reveal its significance through real numbers:

| **Category**              | **Details**                                                                                         |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Number of users**       | Steam has over 120 million monthly active users (as of 2023).                                      |
| **Game library**          | Steam offers a vast library of over 50,000 games, making it one of the largest gaming platforms.    |
| **Peak concurrent users**  | The platform often sees peaks of over 30 million concurrent users during major game releases and events. |
| **Marketplace**           | Steam has a thriving marketplace where users can buy, sell, and trade in-game items and skins.     |
| **Sales and discounts**   | Steam is famous for its seasonal sales, offering discounts that can reach up to 90% on popular titles. |

These figures underscore Steam's role as a cornerstone of the gaming ecosystem, providing access to games and fostering community engagement.
